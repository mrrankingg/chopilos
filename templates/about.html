About Us

who we are
At Chopilos by Slippery, the items on our menu are carefully curated by our in-house chefs, and are inspired by what we like to call an Afro-fusion cuisine, from our all-day breakfast menu to our sizzling, melt-in-your-mouth lamb chops, as well as a reminder of home in our rice dishes.Not only do we make our mouth-watering meals with fresh ingredients including fresh seafood from the Anuoluwapo seafood market in Victoria Island, we also have a variety of carefully handcrafted cocktails, wine, spirits and champagne to choose from.Whether you are ordering a multi-course meal, grabbing a quick drink at the bar or ordering off our 24-hour delivery menu, Chopilos by Slippery’s upscale yet casual ambience provides the perfect space to enjoy a great and comfortable dining time with family and friends as well as clients and business associates.Within five years of its opening, Chopilos by Slippery has been recognized for its quality of food and for reinventing itself to ensure excellent service at all times.We have had notable celebrities including world renowned musicians, Wizkid and Davido, walk through our doors and enjoying our delicious meals at different times.Why don’t you come dine with us and enjoy the tastiest meals you can find in Lagos. We look forward to welcoming you to our exquisite restaurant where you are guaranteed a positively memorable experience. 24-hour delivery menu
